1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use LaynRider and LaynDriver. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We collect your name, email address, phone number, and unique username.

Location Data:

  • Riders: We collect GPS data to facilitate pickup requests and booking accuracy.
  • Drivers: We collect background location data. This means we track your location even when the app is closed or not in use, provided you have set your status to "Active/Online." This is necessary so Riders can track your approach in real-time.

Communication Data: We store chat logs between Riders and Drivers via Google Firebase.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate the connection between Riders and Drivers.
  • To allow Admins (the companies managing drivers) to oversee their fleet.
  • To resolve disputes or reports of harassment.
  • To ensure the technical functionality of the Apps via Google Firebase and Google Maps APIs.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • Google Firebase: Our primary database and authentication provider.
  • Google Maps: Used for navigation and location rendering.
  • Admins: Driver data and location are shared directly with the Admin who registered the driver.
  • Legal Requirements: We may share data if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention: Upon account deletion, user data is archived for a period of 180 days to comply with safety protocols and legal dispute requirements before being permanently purged from Firebase.

Ownership: While data is stored on our Firebase instance, user-generated profile pictures and chat content remain the property of the user, though we retain the right to access this data for moderation and safety purposes.

6. Security

We implement standard security measures provided by Google Firebase to protect your data. However, no electronic transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.
